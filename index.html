About

Lakshya Sivaramakrishnan manages the Women Techmakers Program in India. She is a Data Science enthusiast building tech expertise in Machine Learning and Google Cloud. She has received the "Women in AI Leadership Award" by Analytics India Magazine in 2020 and is a Shenomics "Lead Like a Girl Fellow". She enjoys nurturing communities and empowering women to join, thrive, and lead in the tech industry.

Prior to the current role, she has been a Consultant with Cerner Healthcare and Software Engineer at GE Healthcare. She also holds a GE Analytics India Certification and is an #IAmRemarkable facilitator. She has delivered 225+ talks and workshops on technical topics and applied skills as a part of communities, institutes and organisations. She has completed her Masters in Information Technology from International Institute of Information Technology, Bangalore and Bachelors in Computer Science from Sir M Visvesvaraya Institute of Technology.
